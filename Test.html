Pennant Hills High School

Among Us

Welcome to Pennant Hills High School, located in New South Wales, Australia. We are a leading high school that provides quality education to students from diverse backgrounds. Our dedicated staff and excellent facilities ensure a nurturing and stimulating learning environment.

Our Programs

At Pennant Hills High School, we offer a wide range of programs to cater to the individual needs and interests of our students. These include:

  • Academic Excellence Program
  • Sports and Physical Education
  • Performing Arts
  • STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)
  • Community Service

Facilities

Our school boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including:

  • Modern classrooms equipped with the latest technology
  • Science and computer laboratories
  • Library and resource center
  • Performing arts center
  • Sports fields and gymnasium
